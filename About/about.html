About Us:

Here at Starks' Fantastic Creatures we pride ourselves on providing a safe, fun, and tolerant place to post, and rate your favorite fantasy/sci-fi creatures, characters, or people. A part of the same company that brought you the services of Starks Fantastic Pet Supply, we have a love of all creatures great and small, magical and non magical. We take our environment VERY seriously, and all those who threaten this place of fun and joy, will be subject to a "visit" from our team of specially trained Dire-Wolf Knights. So rude, intolerant, killjoys beware.
